About Us

Question Detection System (QDS) is a revolutionary tool designed to help students efficiently prepare for exams by analyzing past papers and predicting important questions. Our mission is to empower students to study smarter, not harder.

Developed by a team of dedicated educators and technologists, QDS leverages the latest in AI to offer personalized study recommendations, ensuring students can focus on what matters most.
